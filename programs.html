Programs

Our programs support Selective, HSC and IB students from Year 3 to Year 12. We work with a small number of families at a time so that tutors can stay close to each student.

Selective pathway
Opportunity Class and Selective High School

Structured support for OC, Selective and scholarship style exams for primary students.

Years 3 to 6 Small intake
  • Reading comprehension and vocabulary for high level passages.
  • Problem solving and numerical reasoning drills.
  • Exam strategies for timing and question selection.
HSC pathway
HSC Mathematics and Sciences

One to one and very small group sessions for students targeting strong band outcomes in senior courses.

Years 10 to 12 Tutor ranked first
  • Support in Advanced, Extension One and Extension Two Mathematics.
  • Physics and Chemistry taught by top ranked internal students.
  • Past paper habit and marked solutions for trial and HSC level questions.
IB pathway
IB subjects and core

Targeted support in IB HL subjects and guidance for the core, including TOK, CAS and the Extended Essay.

Years 11 and 12 IB 45 tutors
  • HL subject support in Biology, Physics, Mathematics AA and English Literature.
  • Planning and structure for TOK essay and exhibition within IB rules.
  • Step by step planning for the Extended Essay from topic to final draft.
