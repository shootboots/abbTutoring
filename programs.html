How lessons work

Each program follows a simple process so that students know what to expect and parents can see progress over the term.

Start the process
1
Consultation and placement

We speak with parents about goals, current school results and exam timelines. Students are matched with a tutor who suits their pathway and temperament.

2
Diagnostic and plan

The first lesson includes a short diagnostic task. The tutor then builds a simple term plan that balances content, exam skills and past papers.

3
Weekly lessons and homework

Students attend regular lessons and complete targeted homework. Work is marked with specific feedback on method, not just final answers.

4
Review and adjust

After school assessments and exam blocks, the tutor reviews results with the family. The plan is updated to keep students on track for their next deadline.
